AT ESRG, WE THINK OF OURSELVES AS FIREFIGHTERS WORKING FOR FIREFIGHTERS.

That's because - while we possess over 200 years of combined experience in firefighting, fire investigation, engineering and risk assessment, and energy storage safety and testing - ESRG is, at its core, a group of fire service veterans who are passionate about safety. In fact, our founders began as firefighers!
