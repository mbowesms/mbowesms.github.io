New York City has seen an increased demand for energy storage over the past years, and the market sees no sign of slowing down.

From 2018 to 2019, there has been over a 400% increase in interconnection applications, according to Con Edison.








Brooklyn has seen the greatest demand due to increasing strain on the electric grid.

29 applications for Brooklyn-based ESS projects were submitted in 2019, and the number is expected to continue to grow through 2020.
