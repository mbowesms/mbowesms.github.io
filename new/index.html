In order to combat climate change and meet New York City's 2025 energy storage goals,

the Sustainable CUNY Smart DG Hub has teamed with the NYC Mayor's Office of Sustainability (MOS), the NYC Fire Department (FDNY), Department of Buildings (DOB), and Con Edison to provide real-time progress tracking of all energy storage projects in the Five Boroughs.

Learn more

City Storage Goals

New York City has committed to deploying 500 MW of energy storage by 2025.

Con Edison looks to provide 300 MW of the total City Goal.

City Solar Goals

In addition, the City looks to install 1000 MW of solar generating capacity by 2030.

State Storage Goals

New York State has committed to deploying 3 GW of energy storage by 2030.
