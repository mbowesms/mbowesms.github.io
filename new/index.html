ABOUT

In order to combat climate change and meet New York City's 2030 energy storage goals,

the Sustainable CUNY Smart DG Hub has teamed with the NYC Mayor's Office of Sustainability (MOS), NYC Fire Department (FDNY), NYC Department of Buildings (DOB) and Con Edison to provide real-time progress tracking of all energy storage projects in the Five Boroughs.

Learn more

500 MW by 2025

New York City has committed to deploying 500 MW of energy storage by 2025.

Con Edison looks to provide 300 MW of the total City Goal.

1000 MW Solar by 2030

In addition, the City looks to install 1000 MW of solar generating capacity by 2030.

New York State Goals

New York State has committed to deploying 3 GW of energy storage by 2030.

"There is a growing appetite for storage and storage paired with intermittent [renewables] to meet peak demand. That won't change; that will only increase." -Jason Doling, NYSERDA
